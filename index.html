A heat pump is an electrical device that transfers heat from one place to another. It can be used for heating or cooling, depending on the direction of the transfer. Heat pumps are typically more energy-efficient than other heating and cooling options because they move heat instead of generating it.

There are a few different types of heat pumps, but the most common type used in homes is an air-source heat pump. This type of heat pump uses the air outside to transfer heat to or from your home
